This is Coach Micheal Krzyzewski. Micheal Krzyzewski is the head coach of the Duke Mens Basketball team. He has been coaching at Duke for 34 years straight and he is now 71 years old. Coach K has led the Duke Basketball team to 5 NCAA Championships, including one in 2015. He also led the Duke team to 12 final fours apperances, 12 ACC regular season titles and 14 ACC championships. Not only does Coach K coach the Duke Basketball team, he also is the coach of the USA Mens National Basketball Team. He has led this USA team to three olympic gold medals in the three most recent olympics. As the coach of the team USA team, he also coaches the NBA all star game sometimes. Coach K has won many awards with the Duke Basketball team, including becoming the coach with the most wins in NCAA Divison I Basketball History. He has gotten 23 people into the NBA, which is the most in NCAA history.
